Welcome

Student, a building block of an institution plays various roles during their career construction, and to make their jounrney smooth we designed a system which comprises their details. The main issue for a student in their college life is that they fail to manage studies and extra curricular activities simultaneously. In order to make it a bit easy we have designed this system. In this system we take the details of participation in various activites and provide them a facility to get those details approved by faculties in-charge. This way the students can have a track over their participation and also they can upload their certificates or related documents on the portal. This would help them in gaining bonus attendence and also at the time of preparing resume. WE WELCOME YOU TO OUR PORTAL!!
